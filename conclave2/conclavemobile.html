The Manipal Conclave

Each year, in TechTatva, The Manipal Conclave comes to the rescue by bringing together an array of distinguished professionals from varying fields to speak about their work. The Conclave, thus enlightens students, and encourages them to wander beyond their course. The set of speakers at The Manipal Conclave simply gets better every passing year.
